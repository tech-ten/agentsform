## How to Learn

### Step 1: Pick a Subject
Click on **Maths** to start learning.

### Step 2: Choose a Topic
You'll see topics like:
- Numbers and Algebra
- Measurement and Geometry
- Statistics and Probability

Pick the one you want to learn!

### Step 3: Pick a Chapter
Each topic has chapters. For example, Numbers might have:
- Place Value
- Fractions
- Decimals
- Multiplication

### Step 4: Read the Notes
Before the quiz, you'll see notes that explain the topic. Read them carefully! There are examples to help you understand.

### Step 5: Take the Quiz
Answer the questions! Don't worry if you get some wrong - that's how you learn.

## Getting Help from the AI Tutor

If you don't understand something, you can ask for help!

### How to Ask for Help

1. Click on **AI Tutor** or **Ask for Help**
2. Type your question, like:
   - "I don't understand fractions"
   - "How do I multiply big numbers?"
   - "Why is 3/4 bigger than 1/2?"
3. The AI Tutor will explain it to you!

### Tips for Asking Good Questions

‚úÖ **Good questions:**
- "I don't understand why my answer was wrong"
- "Can you explain this in a different way?"
- "What's an easier way to solve this?"

‚ùå **Not so helpful:**
- "Just tell me the answer" (you won't learn this way!)
- "I hate maths" (the tutor can't help with this)

## Taking an Exam

Want to challenge yourself? Try an exam!

1. Click on **Exam Mode**
2. Choose your subject
3. You'll have **20 minutes** to answer **20 questions**
4. No help from the AI tutor during the exam
5. At the end, you'll see your score and which ones you got wrong

Exams are like practice tests for NAPLAN or selective school tests.

## Tips to Get Better at Learning

### 1. Practice Every Day
Even 15 minutes a day helps! That's like watching one YouTube video.

### 2. Don't Skip the Notes
Reading the notes before the quiz helps you get more answers right.

### 3. Ask for Help
If you don't understand, ask the AI tutor. That's what it's there for!

### 4. Learn from Mistakes
When you get something wrong, read the explanation. Next time you'll get it right!

### 5. Keep Your Streak Going
Try not to miss a day. Your streak is like a high score!
